Why is Morpheus Needed?

For Teachers

Enable seamless communication with deaf and hard-of-hearing students in real-time. No need for additional sign language interpreters, making education more accessible and inclusive. Improve classroom dynamics and ensure every student can participate fully in discussions.

For Deaf and Hard-of-Hearing Students

Experience greater independence in educational settings. Participate in classroom discussions naturally without waiting for interpreter assistance. Access immediate translation of sign language to text, making communication with peers and teachers effortless.

For Educational Institutions

Reduce costs associated with hiring full-time interpreters while providing better accessibility. Create a more inclusive learning environment that attracts diverse students. Comply with accessibility requirements using cutting-edge technology.
